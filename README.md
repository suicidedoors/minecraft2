# Controls

**Movement (WASD + Space/Shift)**

W – Move forward

S – Move backward

A – Move left (strafe)

D – Move right (strafe)

SPACE – Move up

LEFT SHIFT – Move down

**Camera Rotation (Arrow Keys or Mouse)**

UP ARROW – Look down

DOWN ARROW – Look up

LEFT ARROW – Turn left 

RIGHT ARROW – Turn right 

MOUSE MOVEMENT – Freely look around
