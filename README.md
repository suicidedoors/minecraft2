# Controls

**Movement (WASD + Space/Shift)**

**W** – Move forward  
**S** – Move backward  
**A** – Move left 
**D** – Move right 
**SPACE** – Move up  
**LEFT SHIFT** – Move down  

**Camera Rotation (Arrow Keys & Mouse)**

**UP ARROW** – Look down  
**DOWN ARROW** – Look up  
**LEFT ARROW** – Turn left  
**RIGHT ARROW** – Turn right  
**MOUSE MOVEMENT** – Freely look around
